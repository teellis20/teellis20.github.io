About Me

My name is Taylor Ellis. I'm orignally from Cincinnati, Ohio, the home of the Bengals and the Reds. Another thing my hometown is famous for, is their special chili called Skyline, which I miss almost every single day. From Cincinnati, I moved to Charlotte, North Carolina where I met my Fiancee and lived for about 4 years. More recently, we moved to Seattle where we now share our home with a brand new poodle puppy named Sirius!


I have my degree in Psychology, but currently work at Geico as a liability claims adjuster. My hope is to one day make the switch to web and app development and once I retire, teach kids the wonderful ways of coding.


In my spare time I like to play video games, take pictues, go hiking and frequent sporting events.
